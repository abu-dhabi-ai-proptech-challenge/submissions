# Abu Dhabi AI PropTech Challenge — Submissions

**Building the Intelligence Layer for Land, Investment and Communities**

This is where teams submit their projects for the Abu Dhabi AI PropTech Challenge, hosted by **Cursor** and **eVoost AI** at **Hub71, Abu Dhabi**.

## How to submit

> **Submit by opening a GitHub Issue using the [Project Submission form](../../issues/new?template=project-submission.yml).**
> One submission per team, before the announced deadline. The Issue timestamp is your submission time.

### 1. Build in your own repo

Use any repo you control — your own from scratch, or one generated from the [`project-template`](https://github.com/abu-dhabi-ai-proptech-challenge/project-template) ("Use this template"). Public repos preferred; private repos must grant the organizer account access.

### 2. Open the Project Submission Issue

The form asks for:

- **Team name** and **team members**
- **Track** — Land Intelligence, Investment Intelligence, Future Communities, or Decision Intelligence
- **Project summary** — the elevator pitch
- **Problem** — what you're solving and for whom
- **Solution** — how your prototype solves it
- **Tech stack**
- **GitHub repo link** (required)
- **Demo link** — deployed app or 2–3 min video (required)
- **Slides link** (optional)
- **What was built during the hackathon** — be specific; judges read this carefully
- **Setup instructions** — how a judge runs your project

### 3. After submitting

Organizers label your Issue (`submission` + your track) and acknowledge it. You may edit to fix typos or broken links before the deadline; substantive changes after the deadline are not considered.
